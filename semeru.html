🧭 Beginner & Master Recommendation

👶 Beginner: STRONGLY RECOMMENDED

  • 🛌 Bring Sleeping Bag Sodium Acetate (extreme cold)
  • 🧥 Thick jacket & layered clothing
  • 🩹 Complete first aid kit
  • 🥤 Extra water supply
  • 🍫 High-calorie emergency food
  • 🔦 Headlamp / flashlight

🧗 Master: RECOMMENDED

  • 🛌 Sleeping bag recommended for safety
  • 🎒 Complete hiking gear
  • ⚠️ Be aware of extreme terrain & long distance
⚠️ Notice
- Extreme cold at summit
- Volcanic eruption (Jonggring Saloko)
- Long tracking distance
- High fatigue risk

🚑 Hypothermia First Aid

  • ❄️ Recognize symptoms: intense shivering, confusion, weakness
  • 🛑 Stop hiking & move to a safer, warmer spot
  • 🧥 Add multiple warm layers immediately
  • 🛌 Use Sleeping Bag to retain body heat
  • 🔥 Warm slowly (avoid direct extreme heat)
  • ⚠️ Contact SAR team / evacuate if condition worsens
