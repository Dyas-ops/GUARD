🧭 Beginner & Master Recommendation

👶 Beginner: STRONGLY RECOMMENDED

  • 🛌 Bring Sleeping Bag Sodium Acetate (cold night temperature)
  • 🧣 Mask / respirator (volcanic ash & gas)
  • 🩹 Complete first aid kit
  • 🥤 Extra water supply
  • 🔦 Headlamp / flashlight

🧗 Master: RECOMMENDED

  • 🛌 Sleeping bag recommended for safety
  • 🧣 Mask / protection gear
  • ⚠️ Monitor volcanic activity & weather updates
⚠️ Notice
- Active volcano (frequent eruptions)
- Hot gas & ash exposure
- Rapid weather changes
- Restricted zones near summit

🚑 Hypothermia First Aid

  • ❄️ Recognize symptoms: shivering, confusion, loss of coordination
  • 🛑 Move away from exposed & windy volcanic areas
  • 🧥 Add warm layers and protect from ash & gas
  • 🛌 Use Sleeping Bag to maintain body heat
  • 🔥 Warm gradually (warm drink if possible)
  • ⚠️ Contact SAR team / evacuate if condition worsens
