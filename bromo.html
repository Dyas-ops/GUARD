🧗 Master: RECOMMENDED

  • 🧥 Light warm layer
  • ⚠️ Adjust based on wind & weather condition
⚠️ Notice
- Strong wind & dust
- Sudden temperature drop at dawn
- Volcanic activity area

🚑 Hypothermia First Aid

  • ❄️ Recognize symptoms: shivering, confusion, fatigue
  • 🛑 Move to a sheltered area (avoid cold wind & dust)
  • 🧥 Add warm layers (jacket, scarf)
  • 🛌 Use Sleeping Bag to maintain body heat
  • 🔥 Warm gradually (warm drink if available)
  • ⚠️ Contact SAR team / evacuate if condition worsens
