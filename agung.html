🧭 Beginner & Master Recommendation

👶 Beginner: STRONGLY RECOMMENDED

  • 🛌 Bring Sleeping Bag Sodium Acetate (cold & windy summit)
  • 🧥 Thick jacket & windproof layer
  • 🩹 Complete first aid kit
  • 🥤 Extra water supply
  • 🍫 High-calorie emergency food
  • 🔦 Headlamp / flashlight

🧗 Master: RECOMMENDED

  • 🛌 Sleeping bag recommended for safety
  • 🎒 Standard hiking gear
  • ⚠️ Be aware of steep terrain & strong wind
⚠️ Notice
- Steep & long trekking route
- Extreme fatigue risk
- Sacred area (restricted zones)
- Sudden weather changes

🚑 Hypothermia First Aid

  • ❄️ Recognize symptoms: shivering, fatigue, confusion
  • 🛑 Move to a sheltered area (avoid strong wind exposure)
  • 🧥 Add warm & windproof layers
  • 🛌 Use Sleeping Bag to maintain body heat
  • 🔥 Warm gradually (warm drink if available)
  • ⚠️ Contact SAR team / evacuate if condition worsens
