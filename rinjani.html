🚑 Hypothermia First Aid

  • ❄️ Recognize symptoms: shivering, confusion, extreme fatigue
  • 🛑 Move to a warmer place & avoid direct wind
  • 🧥 Replace wet clothes with dry layers
  • 🛌 Use Sleeping Bag Sodium Acetate to stabilize body temperature
  • 🔥 Warm gradually (warm drink if conscious)
  • ⚠️ Contact SAR team / evacuate if condition worsens
